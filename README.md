# Beacon  
## A Suicide Prevention and Mental Health Awareness Website

## Abstract
Beacon is a web-based platform developed to promote mental health awareness and suicide prevention, with a primary focus on supporting teenagers experiencing emotional distress. The website provides access to verified suicide prevention helplines, mental health resources, and real-life survivor stories. The project aims to create a safe, simple, and supportive digital space that encourages individuals to seek help and understand that they are not alone.

---

## Introduction
Mental health issues among teenagers are a growing concern in modern society. Emotional stress, anxiety, loneliness, and suicidal thoughts often remain unaddressed due to stigma and lack of awareness. Beacon addresses this challenge by offering a calm and accessible platform that provides guidance, awareness, and support through reliable resources and inspiring survivor stories.

---

## Objectives
- To promote mental health awareness among teenagers  
- To provide easy access to suicide prevention helpline information  
- To share real-life stories of suicide survivors to inspire hope  
- To reduce stigma associated with mental health and seeking help  
- To create a simple, user-friendly, and supportive web interface  

---

## Scope of the Project
This project focuses on mental health awareness and suicide prevention. It is intended for educational and informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical or psychological care. Beacon serves as a supportive platform that encourages users to reach out for help when needed.

---

## Features
- Clean and minimal user interface

- Suicide prevention helpline details
