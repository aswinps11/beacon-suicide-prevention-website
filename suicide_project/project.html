About Beacon


Beacon is a safe space for teenagers facing stress, depression, loneliness, or suicidal thoughts. Our goal is to provide support, resources, and hope to every teen who feels lost or overwhelmed.


  • We care about mental health and emotional well-being.
  • Support is available anytime — you are not alone.
  • We connect teens with resources, professional help, and a supportive community.
  • We spread awareness to reduce stigma around mental health.
